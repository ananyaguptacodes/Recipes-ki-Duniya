Fluffy Pancakes Recipe 🥞

Ingredients:

✅ Dry Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt

✅ Wet Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp melted butter (or oil)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 egg (or ¼ cup yogurt for eggless)

✅ For Cooking:

  • Butter or oil (for greasing the pan)

Steps to Make Pancakes:

1️⃣ Prepare the Batter:

  • In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • In another bowl, whisk milk, melted butter, vanilla extract, and egg.
  • Slowly add the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  • Gently mix (don’t overmix; small lumps are okay).

2️⃣ Heat the Pan:

  • Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over low-medium heat.
  • Lightly grease with butter or oil.

3️⃣ Cook the Pancakes:

  • Pour ¼ cup of batter onto the pan.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles form on the surface.
  • Flip and cook the other side for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.

4️⃣ Serve & Enjoy!

  • Stack them up and drizzle with honey, maple syrup, Nutella, or fruits! 🍯🍓🍫

Crispy French Fries Recipe 🍟

Ingredients:

  • ✅ 3 large potatoes (Russet or any starchy potatoes)
  • ✅ 3 cups cold water (for soaking)
  • ✅ ½ tsp salt (for soaking)
  • ✅ 3 tbsp cornstarch (for extra crispiness)
  • ✅ ½ tsp salt (for seasoning)
  • ✅ ½ tsp black pepper (optional)
  • ✅ Oil for frying (vegetable/sunflower oil)

Steps to Make Crispy French Fries:

1️⃣ Peel & Cut the Potatoes:

  • Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips (about ¼ inch thick).

2️⃣ Soak in Cold Water:

  • Place the fries in a bowl of cold water.
  • Add ½ tsp salt and let them soak for 30 minutes (removes excess starch).

3️⃣ Drain & Dry:

  • Drain the fries and pat them completely dry using a kitchen towel.

4️⃣ Coat with Cornstarch:

  • Sprinkle cornstarch over the fries and toss them gently.
  • This helps create extra crispiness when frying!

5️⃣ First Fry (Blanching):

  • Heat oil to 325°F (160°C) in a deep pan.
  • Fry the potatoes in small batches for 3-4 minutes (don’t let them brown).
  • Remove and drain on a paper towel.

6️⃣ Second Fry (Crisping Up!):

  • Increase oil temperature to 375°F (190°C).
  • Fry the pre-cooked fries again for 2-3 minutes until golden and crispy.
  • Drain excess oil on paper towels.

7️⃣ Season & Serve:

  • Sprinkle with salt & black pepper (or your favorite seasoning).
  • Serve hot with ketchup, cheese sauce, or garlic mayo! 🍅🧄🧀


Bread Pakoda Recipe (Crispy & Tasty!)🍞🥔

Ingredients:

✅ For the Stuffing:

  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Salt to taste

✅ For the Batter:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed to make a thick batter)

✅ Other:

  • 4-5 slices of bread (white/brown)
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps to Make Bread Pakoda:

1️⃣ Prepare the Stuffing:

  • Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds.
  • Add mashed potatoes, green chilies, and all the spices.
  • Mix well, cook for 2 minutes, and turn off the flame.
  • Let the stuffing cool down.

2️⃣ Prepare the Batter:

  • In a bowl, mix besan, ajwain, chili powder, turmeric, baking soda, and salt.
  • Add water gradually and whisk to make a smooth, thick batter.

3️⃣ Assemble the Pakoda:

  • Take a bread slice and spread the potato stuffing evenly.
  • Place another bread slice on top (like a sandwich) and press gently.
  • Cut it into triangles.

4️⃣ Fry the Pakodas:

  • Heat oil in a deep pan.
  • Dip each stuffed bread piece in the besan batter, coat it well.
  • Fry in medium-hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove and drain excess oil on tissue paper.

5️⃣ Serve & Enjoy!

  • Serve hot with green chutney or tamarind chutney and a cup of chai! ☕✨
